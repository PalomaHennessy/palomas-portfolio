About Me

I am a UX designer based in Seattle, WA. My goal as a designer is to create web experiences that are aesthetically pleasing while easy and intuitive to use. I hope to find a culture fit where collaboration and creativity is of utmost importance.
Currently I am working on the platform redesign for Teachers on Reserve , but in my spare time you can probably find me running, traveling or watching Parks and Recreation.

About My Projects

My work consists of both full stack web applications that I created during a 12 week intensive web development course, as well as work I completed as a UX/UI designer.
Projects include technologies such as HTML/CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby on Rails, a MEAN stack, Freewall, bootstrap and materialize.
I hope to expand on these projects and create more in the near future!
