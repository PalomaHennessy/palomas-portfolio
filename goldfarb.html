Goldfarb Educational Consulting

This was a short term contract project. The client is in the education consulting business and wanted a website that would have his contact information, allow for clients to easily connect with him and give some insight into what kind of services he could provide. Additionally the client wanted something more modern and professional than other educational websites. I worked to try and encorporate the clients ask for modern while keeping in mind that this was still for educational purposes.

Technologies Used

  • Adobe Experience Design
  • MongoDB
  • Express
  • Angular
  • Node
  • HTML/CSS/Javascript
  • Bootstrap
