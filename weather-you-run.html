Weather You Run


Development

Built with: Angular, MongoDB, Express, Node, Bootstrap & Open Weather Map API.
Project Details

  • Project: Final Project at General Assembly
  • Date: December, 2015
  • Online: weatheryourun.herokuapp.com/

At General Assembly, our final project was to create a full stack app that was about something we care about. I was inspired to make this app because of my love of runnning. I had always wanted an app that combined the forcast, suggested apparel depending on the weather, and run tracking. At the time I hadn't come across such an app so I used this chance to create one.
