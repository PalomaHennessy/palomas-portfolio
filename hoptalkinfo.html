Hop Talk

The Pacific Northwest is known for having an abundance of beer. However, with so many different beers in the area I couldn't keep track of what I had tried and what I liked. This app was created to keep track for me. Besides learning how to use boostrap and a database for the first time, I learned the importance of being able to be flexible. It started as a app for keeping track of local wines but finding an open database was more difficult than I had anticipated. I quickly had to alter my game plan to finish on time.

Technologies Used

  • MongoDB
  • Express
  • Angular
  • Node
  • HTML/CSS/Javascript
  • Bootstrap
  • BreweryDB
